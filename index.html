Boys To Men

Guiding teenage boys in DeKalb County to be men of God...

Boys to Men was founded in 2000. Our work is with middle school and high school aged boys in Central DeKalb County.

We operate under the Atlanta Youth Project, a non-denominational Christian youth organization.
Mission Statement

...To guide young men towards a greater dependence on God through a personal relationship with Jesus Christ and to challenge them academically, physically and socially.
